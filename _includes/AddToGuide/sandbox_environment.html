We have provided five sets of synthetic credentials for use in the sandbox, corresponding to various amounts of synthetic beneficiaries. The sandbox environment contains all of the same endpoints, resource types, and parameters and production. You may complete all of the same operations as in production. However, sandbox data is not currently updated as frequently as your real data.
